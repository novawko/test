About World

Jovalan is the fourth planet from Kapla-19 (their sun) and the only astronomical object known to harbor life in this solar system.

While large volumes of water can be found throughout the Solar System, only Jovalan sustains liquid surface water. About 82% of Jovalan's surface is made up of the ocean and it mainly consists of isles, islands and rarely ever continents. Most of Jovalans creatures revolve and have evolved around the ocean, due to this.

Jovalan is the homeworld of the species, Kanpolos! Alongside them are many other species, plus some otherworldly species that may visit! Sometimes the species in general may not even visit at all! Jovalan is 3x larger than Earth.
